## 0. Learnings carried forward (fold-in from Alphabet `-07` + Math `-08` + Farver `-09`)

1. **The art-loader pattern is verbatim.** A per-section `index.ts` eager-`import.meta.glob('./*.webp', {eager,
   query:'?url', import:'default'})`, filename-stem тЖТ key, exports a sync `xxxArt(id): string | undefined`; empty
   until files land, then auto-registers. Ordleg copies `src/assets/games/math/index.ts` (the id-keyed variant) to
   `src/assets/games/ordleg/index.ts` тЖТ `ordlegArt(id)`. Filenames = the object **art id** (ASCII; alias the
   Danish-only glyphs тАФ `aeg`/`raev`/`baer`/`loeg`/`aal`/`soe` for ├жg/r├жv/b├жr/l├╕g/├еl/s├╕ тАФ per scene-assets.md).
2. **`PromptStage` (the frosted card) is retired per game, not globally.** The Foundation's engine-level swap only
   reached the shared engines. Hand-rolled games that render `PromptStage` **directly** still show the old frosted
   card and must migrate to `PromptFocus` by hand. In Ordleg the only direct `PromptStage` caller is **Sig et Ord**
   (`SpeakWordGame.tsx:13`/`497`); Stav Ordet renders its picture as a **bare emoji in the body** (no `PromptStage`)
   and L├жs Ordet rides the engine (already on `PromptFocus`). `PromptStage.tsx` **stays in-tree** after this area
   (English `-11` still imports `HeroArt`/`HeroEmoji` from it, and `EnglishLearning`/`StickerAlbum` still carry the
   legacy `#ECF1F8` gradient) тАФ do **not** delete it.
3. **Consolidate/curate to ONE shared subject set.** Alphabet merged two divergent manifests; math baked one counting
   set; farver baked one curated colour-object set. Ordleg bakes **one shared word-picture set** (┬з4) keyed by word,
   reused by L├жs Ordet (answer pictures), Stav Ordet (prompt picture), and Sig et Ord (optional match-bloom).
4. **Picture-less / abstract things stay honest.** Numerals stayed glyphs; math's pattern dots + Nuancer's тШАя╕П/ЁЯМЩ chip
   stayed emoji (abstract tokens). **Ordleg's analogue: the WORDS and LETTERS are the lesson and stay TYPE.** The
   uppercase prompt word (L├жs Ordet), the letter tiles + slots (Stav Ordet), and the spelled-out letters (Sig et Ord)
   are **Comic Sans `Typography`, never baked art** тАФ recognising/reading/spelling the glyphs IS the exercise. Baked
   art is ONLY the *depicted picture* (the cow/car/cat). And **abstract words with no clean depiction** (hej/arm/ben/
   fod/hul/mor/far in Stav's pool) stay **emoji** via the art-gated fallback тАФ never a forced awkward clay render.
5. **Screenshot/verify gotcha:** auto-played game TTS doesn't fire headless тАФ identify the showing subject via the
   baked `<img>` src (`[data-prompt-focus] img`, or the answer-tile `<img>` for L├жs Ordet) and drive feedback with the
   engine's `?fx=correct|wrong|hint` harness (L├жs Ordet rides `UnifiedQuizGame`'s `devFx`). Stav/Sig are hand-rolled
   with no `?fx=` harness тАФ drive them by simulating taps / a scripted STT result (see ┬з8).
6. **Frozen-world payoff (F3) is claimed by removing occluders.** Alphabet/math/farver got calmer by letting the
   softened world show through. Ordleg's occluders are milder (no opaque framed boards), but Sig et Ord's frosted
   `PromptStage` card is a hard occluder over the focal zone тАФ retiring it (┬з3.5) is what makes that board read
   "calm-present & grounded."
7. **The one remaining shared-engine gap.** Alphabet/Math/English put baked art in the **hero/prompt**
   (`questionVisual.art` тЖТ `HeroArt`); the answer tiles were always glyphs. **L├жs Ordet is the inverse** тАФ the prompt
   is the WORD (glyph) and the *answers are the pictures*. `UnifiedQuizGame` has **no baked-art path on answer tiles**
   today (they always render `<Typography>{item.display}</Typography>`). This PRD adds that path (┬з3.1) тАФ a small,
   additive, backward-compatible change (all other quizzes leave `QuizItem.art` unset тЖТ render identically).

### 3.0 The Ordleg re-material rule (READ FIRST тАФ governs every surface below)

Ordleg has two surface families:
- **Picture surfaces** (L├жs Ordet answer pictures, Stav Ordet's prompt picture, Sig et Ord's optional match-bloom):
  become **baked soft-3D word-pictures** тАФ a `<img src={ordlegArt(id)}>` rendered with `softShadow` (a `HeroArt`-style
  cut-out object). On the answer tiles they sit inside the `TactileTile` clay surface (the tile is the grounded
  pressable; the picture is its content); as a prompt they rest in `PromptFocus`'s light-pool + contact shadow.
  **Fallback = today's emoji** while art is un-baked, or for abstract words that are never baked (┬з0.4).
- **Glyph surfaces** (the L├жs Ordet prompt WORD, Stav Ordet's letter tiles + slots + spelled letters, Sig et Ord's
  spelled letters): **stay Comic Sans `Typography`** тАФ recognising/reading/spelling the letterforms IS the lesson.
  They get the tactile **grounding** re-material (contact shadow + soft shadow + press, via `TactileTile`) but the
  content is always type, **never** baked art.

This is why Ordleg can't "wrap everything in `HeroArt`": glyph surfaces need `TactileTile`'s depth + press, not a
picture. **Reduced-motion:** motion off, but baked art, contact shadows, light-pools, feedback rings, and audio all
still render (Foundation parity).

### 3.1 Shared-engine change тАФ baked art on quiz ANSWER tiles (the one new capability, ┬з0.7)

`UnifiedQuizGame` renders every answer tile as `<Typography>{item.display}</Typography>` (720-733); the only baked-art
path is the hero (`questionVisual.art` тЖТ `HeroArt`, 533). L├жs Ordet needs art on the **answers**. Add a small,
additive, backward-compatible path:

- **`QuizItem`**: add `art?: string` (the resolved WebP url for THIS option's picture тАФ distinct from
  `questionVisual.art`, which is the *prompt's* art). Interface at `UnifiedQuizGame.tsx:57-67`.
- **Answer-tile render** (`showOptions.map`, 700-735): when `item.art` is set, render a **`TileArt`** `<img>` as the
  tile's child instead of the `<Typography>`; otherwise the existing `Typography` (glyph/emoji) тАФ unchanged. Keep the
  `AnswerTile` wrapper, `tileStateFor`/`tileHintFor`, `disabled`, and the entrance `motion.div` exactly as-is (the
  picture is just the tile's *content*).
- **`TileArt`**: a tile-scaled sibling of `HeroArt` (add it to `PromptStage.tsx` next to `HeroArt`, or inline a tiny
  component). `<img>` with `objectFit:'contain'`, `maxWidth/maxHeight:'100%'`, `height:'clamp(2.6rem, 12vh, 5rem)'`
  (fills the 4:3 answer tile with padding), `filter: softShadow(1)`, `draggable={false}`, `aria-hidden`,
  `pointerEvents:'none'`, `userSelect:'none'`, a `PHONE_LANDSCAPE` height clamp (mirror `HeroArt`'s phone rule).
- **Backward-compat / blast radius:** only L├жs Ordet sets `QuizItem.art`; alphabet.quiz, math counting/patterns, and
  the three English quizzes never set it тЖТ they render byte-identically. This is a **shared-engine edit** тАФ ┬з8 verifies
